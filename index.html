Welcome to Care For NGO

Our not-for-profit organization works to empower our elderly across India. We strive to make a better place for our elderly. We work with 1000s of volunteers to get nutrition, healthcare, protection, education at the time of need. Natural conservation is one of our topmost priorities. As natural resources are getting scarce we push to make this world a better place for our next generation.

Causes We are Serving

Help Senior Citizens

Caring Angels (Senior Citizens) have always looked after their children, but it is time they need care & love from their children. Our objective is to work for Senior Parents. Amcha Ghar children have been regularly visiting nearby old age homes. It has been a very wonderful experience for our kids to spend time with the senior parents, to talk to them, care for them in their small gestures. Due to whatsoever reasons, the senior parents long for the love of their children & grandchildren.

Help Children

Smile Foundation, an NGO for poor child education, is an NGO in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 400 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India.
